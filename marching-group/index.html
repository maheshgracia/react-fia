    1. Only peaceful marching in the Parade, no demonstration or protest would be allowed and anyone found engaging in either of these activities shall be asked to leave the parade immediately.

Copy of the banner/theme must be submitted at least 10 days prior to the parade, and slogans against any religion, caste, country & color through the distribution of the flyer or any form of publicity are prohibited, and anyone found engaging in either of these activities shall be asked to leave the parade immediately.

    7. No extra person or exchange will be permitted, besides the count given by this application.
