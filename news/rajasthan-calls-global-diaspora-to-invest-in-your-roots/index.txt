<p class="my-2 [&amp;+p]:mt-4 [&amp;_strong:has(+br)]:inline-block [&amp;_strong:has(+br)]:pb-2"><em>Bhajanlal Sharma unveils new push to connect overseas Rajasthanis with home state</em></p>
<p class="my-2 [&amp;+p]:mt-4 [&amp;_strong:has(+br)]:inline-block [&amp;_strong:has(+br)]:pb-2">Rajasthan has issued a strong and optimistic call to its global diaspora, urging overseas Rajasthanis, including those in the United States, to reconnect with their roots and actively participate in the state&rsquo;s growth story. Chief Minister Bhajanlal Sharma used the first Pravasi Rajasthani Divas in Jaipur to position the diaspora as key partners in building a modern, prosperous Rajasthan, highlighting both emotional bonds and new investment opportunities.<span data-state="closed">​</span></p>
<p class="my-2 [&amp;+p]:mt-4 [&amp;_strong:has(+br)]:inline-block [&amp;_strong:has(+br)]:pb-2">At the event, the state showcased its recent policy reforms and infrastructure push, noting that MoUs worth trillions of rupees were signed at the Rising Rajasthan investment summit, with a significant portion already progressing on the ground. Sharma emphasized that Rajasthan is emerging as a major investment destination across tourism, renewable energy, water management, and industry, and invited professionals and entrepreneurs of Rajasthani origin to channel their expertise and capital back home.<span data-state="closed">​</span></p>
<p class="my-2 [&amp;+p]:mt-4 [&amp;_strong:has(+br)]:inline-block [&amp;_strong:has(+br)]:pb-2">In a major institutional step, the government has approved a dedicated Non-Resident Rajasthani Policy 2025 and created a Department of Domestic and Overseas Rajasthani Affairs to streamline facilitation, clearances, and grievance redress for global Rajasthanis. New Rajasthan Foundation chapters, including several overseas, and an NRR Investment Facilitation Cell aim to make it easier for the diaspora to explore projects, mentor startups, and support local community initiatives.<span data-state="closed">​</span></p>
<p class="my-2 [&amp;+p]:mt-4 [&amp;_strong:has(+br)]:inline-block [&amp;_strong:has(+br)]:pb-2">These developments open concrete avenues to engage with Rajasthan&rsquo;s growth while preserving cultural and family ties. US-based Rajasthanis can look at sector-focused delegations during future roadshows in New York, as well as partnerships routed through India&rsquo;s consular network, which has been actively promoting deeper diaspora participation in India&rsquo;s development agenda.<span data-state="closed">​</span></p>
