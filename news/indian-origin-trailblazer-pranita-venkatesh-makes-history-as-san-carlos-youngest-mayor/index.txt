f:T6a9,<h2 id="m_-4421769878065664826gmail-a-milestone-for-indo-fijian-diaspora-in-us-leaders">A Milestone for Indo-Fijian Diaspora in US Leadership</h2>
<p>Pranita Venkatesh, a child psychologist of Indian origin born in Fiji, was unanimously sworn in as Mayor of San Carlos, California, on December 8, 2025. This achievement marks her as one of the city's youngest mayors and its first leader of South Asian heritage, inspiring the Indian-American community across the US. San Carlos, a vibrant city of 28,000 residents near Silicon Valley, known as the "City of Good Living,"-benefits from her deep local roots, having lived there since 2009 with her husband and two children.</p>
<p>Venkatesh's journey reflects dedication and community focus. Holding a bachelor's from Notre Dame de Namur University and a master's in early childhood and clinical psychology, she served as a child psychologist in East Palo Alto and a buyer at Macy's before directing Paragon Montessori preschool in San Carlos. Elected to the City Council in 2022 with San Mateo Democratic Party endorsement, she previously contributed three years to the Economic Development Advisory Commission, building a strong foundation for her mayoral role.</p>
<p>Mayor Venkatesh prioritizes public safety, childcare, economic stability, infrastructure, and affordable housing to bridge community needs with effective governance. Her leadership celebrates the city's diversity and showcases the rising influence of Indian-origin professionals in American politics, following figures like New York City Mayor-elect Zohran Mamdani.
