Culture Minister Gajendra Singh Shekhawat expressed gratitude, noting the festival's emotional resonance across generations and its role in promoting light over despair. The event underscores UNESCO's 2003 Convention to safeguard rituals, festivals, and crafts threatened by globalization, fostering worldwide respect for cultural diversity.<span data-state="closed">​</span></p>
