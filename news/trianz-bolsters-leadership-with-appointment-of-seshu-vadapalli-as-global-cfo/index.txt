<p dir="ltr">Trianz has officially named Seshu Vadapalli as its new Global Chief Financial Officer, a strategic move aimed at scaling the company&rsquo;s "Transformation Services as a Software" model. Reporting directly to CEO and founder Sri Manchala, Vadapalli is tasked with steering global financial strategy and capital planning as Trianz aggressively expands its AI-powered platform business.</p>
<p dir="ltr">The appointment comes at a pivotal moment. Trianz has successfully pivoted from traditional IT services to a high-growth software model, with platform revenues currently surging at triple-digit year-over-year rates. <p dir="ltr"><strong>Driving AI Momentum Through Strategic Leadership&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p dir="ltr">Vadapalli&rsquo;s background is tailor-made for this transition. With nearly twenty years in software finance, his resume includes a significant tenure at IBM and leadership roles at AppViewX and Material. An alumnus of IIT Madras and Carnegie Mellon, he specializes in scaling P&amp;L structures and building the financial systems required for global growth.</p>
<p dir="ltr">"We correctly anticipated the needs of the AI era," Manchala noted, emphasizing that Vadapalli&rsquo;s expertise will provide the infrastructure to scale Trianz&rsquo;s recent momentum. With 100 new enterprise clients and 30 fresh patent filings since May 2025, Trianz is positioning itself as a dominant force in the AI-led B2B landscape.</p>
