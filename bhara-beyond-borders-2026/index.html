Welcome to Bharat Beyond Borders 2026. This grand gala celebrates the achievements, culture, and global impact of the Indian diaspora. Join community leaders, dignitaries, and artists for an unforgettable evening.

  • Black Tie & Formal Suits

  • Evening Gowns & Elegant Sarees

  • Indo-Western Glamour

  • Sophisticated & Luxury Styling

  • Casual Wear

  • Sneakers & Sports Shoes

  • Caps, Hoodies & Athleisure

  • Ripped Jeans & Streetwear

Individual tickets can be purchased online through our official portal. Early bird registration opens soon. Please keep your digital confirmation handy at the entrance.

Corporate and VIP tables seating 10 guests are available. For premium placements and group reservations, please contact our hospitality desk directly.

The gala will be hosted at the historic Cipriani. Detailed valet parking instructions, public transit routes, and map links will be shared with ticket holders via email.

The evening features keynote addresses by global leaders, cultural fusion dance performances, award ceremonies recognizing community icons, and live musical entertainment.

Enjoy a multi-course gourmet dinner curated by award-winning chefs. The menu seamlessly blends authentic Indian spices with contemporary global cuisine. Vegetarian and vegan options are fully covered.

Doors open at 6:00 PM. Please arrive early for security screening. A valid government-issued ID matching your ticket registration is required for entry.

Q: Are tickets refundable?
A: No, all ticket sales are final and non-refundable, but they are transferable with prior notification.

Q: Is there an age restriction?
A: This is an adult-focused formal gala event. We recommend guests be 18 years or older.
