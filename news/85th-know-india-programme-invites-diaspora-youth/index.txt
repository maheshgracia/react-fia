<p>Diaspora youth in the United States are invited to experience India like never before through the prestigious 85th edition of the Know India Programme (KIP), scheduled for January 6&ndash;27, 2026. Designed for Persons of Indian Origin (PIO) aged 21&ndash;35, KIP offers selected participants an immersive journey into India's vibrant culture, economic transformation, and innovations&mdash;all under the hospitality of the Government of India, which covers most travel costs and accommodation. The application window closes on December 8, 2025, so aspirants should act fast to secure their chance for this once-in-a-lifetime exploration.</p>
<p>This year&rsquo;s KIP stands out because it seamlessly integrates participants into the Viksit Bharat Young Leaders Dialogue (VBYLD), the second edition of India&rsquo;s reimagined National Youth Festival. This convergence provides a remarkable forum for global Indian youth delegates to exchange ideas, showcase leadership, and witness India&rsquo;s rapid progress toward &ldquo;Viksit Bharat 2047.&rdquo; Notably, attendees will have the opportunity to attend sessions in the presence of India&rsquo;s Prime Minister, further elevating the significance of their visit.</p>
<p>Selected youth will delve into India&rsquo;s heritage, contemporary society, technological advancements, and evolving role on the world stage. Highlights include hands-on learning, city tours, networking with fellow diaspora and local youth leaders, and attending national-level celebrations.
