Special Events

The list of special events patronized by FIA is exhaustive. However, the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav launch at Capitol Hill, Boston Marathon, Indian Flag Hoisting at Times Square, International Women’s Day, Bihar Diwas, International Yoga Day, Diwali Soup Kitchen, Gujarat-Maharashtra Day and Gala Reception are some of the noted ones have been a regular annual feature. And with every passing year, one or the other marquee events are getting added. Find out yourself and stay tuned for more.
