f:T61a,<p dir="ltr"><strong>Global icon Priyanka Chopra Jonas to be honored with the Global Vanguard Award at the star-studded 2026 Gold Gala</strong></p>
<p dir="ltr">Content: Priyanka Chopra Jonas is set to be a focal point of the fifth annual Gold Gala, where she will be celebrated for her monumental contributions to the global entertainment landscape. Gold House, the prominent collective supporting Asian Pacific leaders, announced that the event will take place on May 9, 2026.</p>
<p dir="ltr">Chopra will receive the prestigious Global Vanguard Honor, joining an elite group of honorees including Jet Li, who will receive the Gold Legend Honor, and Simu Liu, the recipient of the Gold Mogul Honor.</p>
<p dir="ltr"><strong>Priyanka Chopra Honored Amid Global Spotlight and New Projects&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p dir="ltr">The gala, themed "A New Gold World," aims to foster unity and shared abundance among 650 industry pioneers. The evening is expected to be a star-studded affair, with Bowen Yang delivering the opening monologue and a performance by Hayley Kiyoko.</p>
<p dir="ltr">This recognition comes during a prolific period for Chopra. Following her 2025 successes in Heads of State and The Bluff, she is preparing for a highly anticipated return to Indian cinema. She will star as the mysterious Mandakini in SS Rajamouli&rsquo;s upcoming epic, Varanasi.</p>
<p dir="ltr">Set for a 2027 release, the film also features Mahesh Babu and Prithviraj Sukumaran, marking Chopra&rsquo;s first major collaboration with the RRR director.</p>
