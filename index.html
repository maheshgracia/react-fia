AboutFederation Of Indian Associations

The Federation of Indian Associations (FIA) is a non-profit organization established in 1990 to represent the Indian diaspora in the Northeast USA. It has engaged and enhanced the community through various initiatives for over five decades, promoting and safeguarding the values and culture of the Indian American community. FIA is established in the states of New York, New Jersey, Connecticut, Massachusetts, Rhode Island, New Hampshire, Vermont and Maine in the USA.

FIA's journey since 1970 has been eventful. The Indian-American community has added iconic events to its calendar each year.
