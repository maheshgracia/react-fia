More than 2000+ people attended the event. By participating, attendees learned how to connect with the Sixth Sense— and gain greater clarity, awareness, peace, and more. As part of his multi-city tour across the United States, Gurudev emphasized the significance of mental health awareness and its role in creating violence-free societies.
