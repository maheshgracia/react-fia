f:T5a5,<p dir="ltr"><strong>Advancing India&rsquo;s medical sector through AI-driven drug discovery and strategic healthcare innovation</strong></p>
<p dir="ltr">The Consul General of India in New York, Binaya Srikanta Pradhan, recently met Dr. Siddhartha Mukherjee, the Pulitzer Prize-winning oncologist and founder of Manas AI, for a focused conversation on deploying artificial intelligence to strengthen India's healthcare sector.</p>
<p dir="ltr">Manas AI, co-founded by Mukherjee alongside entrepreneur Reid Hoffman, operates as a full-stack, AI-driven company aimed at discovering next-generation medicines for cancer, autoimmune disorders, and rare conditions.&nbsp;</p>
<p dir="ltr">The startup leverages AI to accelerate the drug discovery pipeline; Mukherjee noted that Manas has already identified compounds previously unimaginable in their chemical composition.</p>
<h3 dir="ltr"><strong>AI Collaboration to Boost India&rsquo;s Healthcare Innovation</strong></h3>
<p dir="ltr">During the meeting, both parties explored pathways through which emerging technologies and AI platforms could be channeled into advancing medical research and healthcare infrastructure across India.&nbsp;</p>
<p dir="ltr">Mukherjee emphasized that AI-guided design holds the potential to materially improve the precision and safety of advanced therapies, a vision closely aligned with India's growing ambitions in health-tech innovation.</p>
