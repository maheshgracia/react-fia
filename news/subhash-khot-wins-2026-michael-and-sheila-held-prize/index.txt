<p dir="ltr">In a major win for the global scientific community, Indian-origin computer scientist Subhash Khot has been awarded the prestigious 2026 Michael and Sheila Held Prize. An alumnus of IIT Bombay and Princeton, Khot is no stranger to elite accolades, counting a MacArthur Fellowship and the Rolf Nevanlinna Prize among his previous honors.&nbsp;</p>
<p dir="ltr">This latest recognition underscores his role as a pivotal figure in discrete optimization.
