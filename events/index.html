FIA Events

  • Gala on the Hudson

    • 20 August @ 5:30 pm - 10:00 pm
    • Gala On The Hudson 2023 Port of Boarding : Pier 40, NY, New York

    The World's Largest India Day Parade 2023 in NYC will be followed by a Grand Gala on the Hudson River in the Cornucopia Majesty, which itself is the largest cruise on the east coast.

    Celebrities, stand-up comedy, Gujaratis' all-time favorite form of entertainment, Garba, and the high-energy dances Bhangra and Dhol will all be featured during the event. Even more musically and enthusiastically, the Gala will feature performances by Indian idol singers.

    Apart from this, there will be DJs and a full-course dinner for everyone present at the event. The Cornucopia will leave the shore at 6:30 p.m. sharp and return at 10 p.m.
